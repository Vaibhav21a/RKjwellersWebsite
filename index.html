ABOUT US

Myself Mr. Rakesh Kumar Verma , a responsible, faithful, and honest wholeseller of Gold Ornaments from Sarafa Bazar, Aligarh. Currently I hold 2 branches in Sarafa Bazar(a vast and Gold Market)

Things were not that shinny in back days, when I was young My granfather leaves a shop in main Sarafa Bazar but at that time recently My father suffered from electric shock and got paralyzed, I was just 14 years old boy who is going to be the man of family as I was the eldest in the family. Things were too bad that my family of 9 live in a conjusted Compartment for years but I stood up strong in that situation, I leave my house to learn the work of Gold & Silver Polish of Old Gold jwellery, which was not introduced yet at that time. I was the first to practice it in my city after mastering it. Due to the family load I could not finish my Graduation. but after some relief when I bought a house for our livings and my brothers started coming to the work , I decided to continue my education , after the completion I got married and named my other shop in the name of my first son, "Vaibhav".
